European congress highlights

Featured presentations

European Society for Paediatric Endocrinology 2024

DCCR therapy demonstrated clinically meaningful reductions in hyperphagia scores and supported improvements in metabolic markers among adolescents with Prader-Willi syndrome in a long-term open-label extension (OLE) analysis.

Key endpoints included changes in Hyperphagia Questionnaire for Clinical Trials (HQ-CT) total score, fasting insulin, and body composition assessed via DXA scans. Data reflect interim findings; DCCR is an investigational therapy not yet approved in the EU.

European privacy commitments

GDPR-aligned processing

Soleno Therapeutics (UK) Ltd acts as the data controller for personal data collected through this website. We process limited professional information to verify healthcare professional status, respond to unsolicited medical inquiries, and manage congress resource access. Data is stored within the EU/UK on ISO 27001-certified infrastructure.

We do not transfer personal data outside the EEA/UK without appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses or UK International Data Transfer Agreement). Aggregate analytics are generated using privacy-enhanced measurement that suppresses individual identifiers.

  • Data minimisation: only professional contact details required.
  • Retention: enquiry records retained for 3 years unless earlier deletion is requested.
  • Rights: access, rectification, objection, restriction, portability, and erasure can be exercised via privacy@soleno.life.
  • Pharmacovigilance: safety information is handled under separate statutory retention rules.

Support for education

Medical grants & investigator-initiated research

Soleno Medical Affairs Europe welcomes submission of proposals that advance scientific understanding of DCCR, rare metabolic diseases, and comprehensive care pathways. Each request is assessed by a cross-functional review committee to ensure alignment with ethical standards and local regulations (EFPIA, ABPI, MedTech Europe).

  • Independent Medical Education: funding for accredited symposia or enduring materials.
  • Investigator-Initiated Research: observational studies, registries, or small interventional trials.
  • Patient Advocacy Collaboration: evidence-based educational initiatives supporting caregivers.

Submission considerations

  • Clearly defined scientific objectives and methodology.
  • Budget detail compliant with fair market value principles.
  • Data privacy safeguards for participant data (pseudonymisation, secure transfer).
  • Transparency: investigators retain publication rights; Soleno requests acknowledgement of support.
